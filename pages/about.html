ABOUT US

Welcome to the right place for a retro mechanical keyboard. At MinimalType, we are passionate about bringing you the best in minimal and customizable mechanical keyboards. Our journey began with a simple love for the tactile feel and distinct sound of mechanical switches, combined with a desire to create keyboards that are not only functional but also aesthetically pleasing. Whether you're a gamer, a coder, or someone who simply enjoys the unique experience of typing on a mechanical keyboard, we have something for you.

Our extensive range of switches, cases, and key caps ensures that you can find the perfect fit for your needs. Plus, with our customization options, you can design a keyboard that truly reflects your style and preferences. Don't waste any more time – discover your next keyboard with us and experience the perfect blend of retro charm and modern innovation.
